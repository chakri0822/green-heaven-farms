About Us

At Green Heaven Farms, we are committed to transforming traditional farming through vertical farming and hydroponics. Our mission is to conserve water and maximize crop yields while promoting sustainable practices.

With innovative irrigation methods like drip systems and rainwater harvesting, we address water conservation challenges posed by climate change and groundwater depletion.
